This is an egg recipe.

the egg dish is made for breakfast. It is a simple recipe that can be made in just a few minutes. the dish is very tasty and can be enjoyed by everyone. It is a great way to start the day and can be served with different breakfast items.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 50 ml soybean oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red pepper powder
  • 1 piece onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. heat the oil in a pan
  2. add the chopped onion, turmeric powder, salt and red pepper powder
  3. crack the eggs into the pan
  4. add the butter
  5. cook for a few minutes until the eggs are set
  6. after cook, add the milk
    7. li>stir the eggs and milk together until well combined
  7. Now serve hot and enjoy!
