Chicken Recipe

This chicken recipe is perfect for a quick and tasty meal. It combines simple ingredients to create a flavorful dish that everyone will love. Whether you're cooking for yourself or for a family, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings for something delicious and comforting.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 250 ml soybean oil
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 piece of onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin and cardamom powder
  • clean water
  • Some mint leaves
  • Some coriander leaves.

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut the chicken into pieces
  2. Heat the soybean oil in a pan
  3. Add the onion, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder and salt for a minute
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until done
  5. Add black pepper to taste
  6. Lastly, add cumin and cardamom powder to taste
  7. add coriander leaves and mint leaves
  8. Then your chicken is ready to serve.
