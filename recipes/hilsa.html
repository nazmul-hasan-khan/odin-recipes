Hilsa Fish Recipe

Hilsa fish, aslo known as Ilish, is a popular fish in Bangladesh and West Bengal. It is known for its unique flavor and is often considered a delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Hilsa fish
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin and cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 5 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 piece of onion, sliced
  • Clean water

Instructions

  1. Wash the Hilsa fish and cut it into pieces.
  2. Apply the oil in a pan and heat it.
  3. Add the salt, sliced onion, turmeric powder and red chili powder.
  4. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  5. Add the hilsa fish pieces and cook until cooked through.
  6. Stir in the lemon juice and serve hot.
